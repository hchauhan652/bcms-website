Common Issues with Revolving Chairs

Revolving chairs experience wear and tear over time. Here are the most common problems users face:

  • Broken Casters/Wheels: Reduced mobility due to damaged or stuck wheels.
  • Gas Lift Malfunction: Chair height cannot be adjusted or sinks when seated.
  • Tilting Mechanism Problems: Chair fails to recline or tilts unevenly.
  • Loose or Damaged Armrests: Wobbly or broken armrests reduce comfort and usability.
  • Backrest Issues: Loss of lumbar support, recline lock failure, or loose backrest.
  • Damaged Upholstery: Torn or worn-out seat and backrest fabric.
  • Base Cracks: Five-star base may crack under prolonged stress or improper use.
