Choosing the Right Upholstery for Your Office Chairs & Sofas

Updated: July 2025 | Category: Furniture & Office Interiors

Upholstery isn't just about looksâ€”it directly impacts the durability, maintenance, and comfort of your office furniture. Whether you're buying new chairs or refurbishing old ones, knowing the differences in materials can save you time, money, and hassle.

1. Leatherette (PU or PVC)

Pros: Affordable, easy to clean, water-resistant, modern appearance.

Cons: Can peel over time with wear, not very breathable in hot environments.

2. Fabric Upholstery

Pros: Comfortable, breathable, available in various textures and patterns.

Cons: Absorbs stains and dust more easily, needs regular cleaning.

3. Mesh Material

Pros: Highly breathable, great for long sitting hours, modern ergonomic appeal.

Cons: Limited cushioning, not ideal for sofa-type seating, can tear if mishandled.

4. Genuine Leather

Pros: Luxurious look and feel, very durable when properly maintained.

Cons: Expensive, requires conditioning, not ideal for humid climates.

5. Velvet & Suede

Pros: Plush appearance, premium comfort, stylish for lounge areas.

Cons: Easily stained, attracts dust, requires professional cleaning.
